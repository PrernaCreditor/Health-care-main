Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our programs and services

What's the best way to get started with VitaPure+?

We recommend beginning with our free wellness assessment to identify which program aligns best with your health goals. From there, you can schedule a complimentary 30-minute consultation with one of our wellness advisors to create your personalized plan.

How quickly can I expect to see results from your programs?

Results vary depending on the program and individual factors, but most clients report noticeable improvements within the first 2-4 weeks. Our Cellular Detox program often shows the most immediate results, while our Longevity Blueprint is designed for gradual, sustainable benefits over 6 months.

Do you accept insurance for your wellness programs?

While we don't directly bill insurance, many of our clients successfully submit for out-of-network reimbursement. We provide detailed receipts with diagnostic codes that may qualify for HSA/FSA payments. Our team can also assist with documentation for insurance claims.

What makes VitaPure+ different from other wellness programs?

Our approach combines cutting-edge scientific testing with ancient wellness wisdom, all delivered through 100% organic protocols. Unlike generic programs, we customize every aspect based on your unique biomarkers, genetics, and lifestyle. Plus, you get 24/7 access to our team of functional medicine practitioners.

Can I do your programs remotely or do I need to visit in person?

All our programs are designed to work remotely! We ship all necessary testing kits and organic supplements directly to you, and consultations are conducted via secure video. Of course, you're always welcome to visit our wellness center if you're in the San Francisco area.
