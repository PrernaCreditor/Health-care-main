Our Story & Vision

Pioneering organic wellness through science, nature, and personalized care

Meet Our Experts Our Approach

Redefining Wellness Through Organic Science

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Amanda Smith, VitaPure+ began as a small wellness clinic with a bold vision: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge science and ancient organic wisdom.

After overcoming her own health challenges through organic protocols, Dr. Smith developed our proprietary 5-Pillar Methodology that has now helped over 15,000 clients worldwide achieve transformative results.

What sets us apart is our commitment to:

  • 100% certified organic protocols
  • Peer-reviewed scientific validation
  • Personalized bio-individual plans
  • Sustainable farm-to-table partnerships
